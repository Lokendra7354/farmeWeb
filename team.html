A farmworker, farmhand or agricultural worker is someone employed for labor in agriculture. In labor law, the term
"farmworker" is sometimes used more narrowly, applying only to a hired worker involved in agricultural production,
including harvesting, but not to a worker in other on-farm jobs, such as picking fruit.

  • Owener of RAJPUT FARMS

MR.OMSINGH RAJPUT is a dedicated farmer whose life revolves around the rhythms of the agricultural cycle and the demands of his land. Born and raised in a farming family, Om Singh inherited not just the land but also the passion and knowledge of generations before him. His farm, nestled in the fertile plains of India, is a testament to his hard work, resilience, and commitment to sustainable farming practices. Om Singh begins his day at the crack of dawn, inspecting his fields and ensuring that his crops are healthy and thriving. His tasks are diverse, ranging from planting seeds and irrigating the fields to weeding and applying natural fertilizers. Om Singh is an advocate of organic farming, believing that sustainable practices not only yield better produce but also preserve the health of the soil and the environment. He employs crop rotation and composting techniques to maintain soil fertility and minimize the use of chemical pesticides.
